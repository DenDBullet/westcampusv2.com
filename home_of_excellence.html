ABOUT

THE HOME OF EXCELLENCE AND HUMANITY:

Unlocking potential, fostering growth, and nurturing
bright minds and shaping future leaders.

A school established by the City of Biñan, under the leadership and vision of MAYOR WALFREDO R. DIMAGUILA, JR. and VICE MAYOR ANGELO B. ALONTE

The Biñan City Senior High School - West Campus is a public school which officially opened last August 2023. The school offers Senior Highschool Academic Track consisting of Strands such as Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics (STEM), Accountancy, Business, and Management (ABM), and Humanities and Social Sciences (HUMSS).

This modern educational facility is created to provide a secure, inspiring, and contemporary learning environment for students from Brgy. Langkiwa and surrounding areas. With state-of-the-art classrooms, laboratories, and facilities, Biñan City Senior High School - West Campus is committed to equipping students with the knowledge, skills, and values necessary for a successful future.

This institution represents Biñan City's dedication to exceptional education, community development, and the enhancement of its people. It embodies our shared goal of fostering a brighter, more prosperous future for every student who walks through its doors.

This establishment stands as a promise to the community, a commitment to invest in the youth, unlock their potential, and prepare them to become leaders and innovators in a constantly changing world.

"Dedicated to the youth of Biñan, inspiring minds, and shaping the future."
