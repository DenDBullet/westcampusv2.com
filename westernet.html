ABOUT

WESTERNET the official website of Biñan City Senior High School - West Campus, is a platform that provides students, parents, teachers, and the community with easy access to important information and updates about the school.

The website offers a variety of content and features, including:

School News: Stay updated on the latest school activities, events, and important announcements.

Announcements: Find essential school notices such as exam schedules, holidays, and other important dates.

Awards and Recognitions: Learn about the achievements of students and faculty in academics, sports, journalism, arts, and other fields.

Events and Activities Discover upcoming events and opportunities for student involvement in school programs.

Research: View research projects and academic studies created by students and faculty.

Contact Information: Easily reach out to teachers, administrators, and staff for any assistance.

The website also features a School Gallery, where visitors can view images of the school’s modern facilities, including classrooms, laboratories, and other amenities. This gallery highlights the school's commitment to providing a safe, inspiring, and well-equipped learning environment.
