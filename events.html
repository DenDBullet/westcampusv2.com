Announcing the Graduation Ball of the Grade 12 Class of 2025: Hollywood Extravaganza

Posted: March 26, 2025

We are thrilled to invite you to a night of glitz, glamour, and unforgettable memories as we celebrate the culmination of an incredible journey.

The Grade 12 students of 2025 cordially invite you to their Graduation Ball on April 12, 2025, where the magic of Hollywood comes alive with the theme of Hollywood Extravaganza.

This evening promises to be an elegant affair, where dreams are realized and memories are made. Step onto the red carpet in your finest attire and embrace the spotlight as we honor the achievements, hard work, and dedication of our graduates. It will be a night of celebration, filled with dazzling moments, sparkling conversation, and a sense of pride in all that has been accomplished.

For further information regarding the time, venue, or any other updates, please stay tuned and follow our official communications. We look forward to celebrating with you at the Hollywood Extravaganza!
